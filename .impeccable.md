## Design Context

### Users
Layman users who are not highly technical, as well as photo shop employees. The primary goal is frictionless ease of access to generate layouts quickly without confusion.

### Brand Personality
Apple iPhone design: Premium, intuitive, effortless.

### Aesthetic Direction
Minimalist visual direction. Must support both Light mode and Dark mode seamlessly.
References: Apple ecosystem design language (clean spacing, refined typography, subtle depth, high legibility, large touch-friendly areas).

### Design Principles
1. **Absolute Clarity**: Eliminate visual noise; every element must have a distinct functional purpose.
2. **Effortless Affordance**: Buttons and inputs should be immediately recognizable and easy to interact with, minimizing cognitive load.
3. **Refined Contrast**: Use subtle shading, ample negative space, and crisp typography to create hierarchy, rather than relying on heavy borders or generic gradients.
4. **Adaptive Context**: Flawless transition between light and dark modes, maintaining the premium feel across both.
